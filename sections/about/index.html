About

I’m Sumaiya Hossain (RCIC #R1041075), a Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant and the founder of Arium Immigration.

I’ve spent my life crossing borders — having grown up in eight different countries, I’ve seen how immigration can shape not just individual lives, but entire families and futures. After years of moving from place to place, I now proudly call Canada home.

My path to this work has been shaped by both personal experience and professional training. I’ve spent years watching how immigration policies impact real people — their dreams, their stability, their sense of belonging. That’s what drew me to this field: the opportunity to stand alongside people as they navigate one of the most important journeys of their lives - whether it’s through studying, working, visiting, reuniting with loved ones or finding a new home.

I hold a Bachelor of Business & Commerce and a Master of International Business from Monash University, and a Master of Development Practice from the University of Waterloo. I also completed the Graduate Diploma in Immigration and Citizenship Law at Queen’s University.

At Arium Immigration, I bring together deep empathy, legal and cross-cultural understanding to offer honest, thorough, and compassionate immigration services. Whether you’re planning a new future or overcoming a complex hurdle, I’m here to help you move forward with clarity and care.
